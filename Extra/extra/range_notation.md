
### üìò **Interval Notation Cheat Sheet with Examples**

| Notation | Meaning                           | Includes?        | Example Numbers                     |
| -------- | --------------------------------- | ---------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| `[0, 1]` | From 0 **to** 1                   | ‚úÖ Includes 0 & 1 | `0, 0.1, 0.5, 0.9999, 1`            |
| `[0, 1)` | From 0 **up to** (but not) 1      | ‚úÖ 0, ‚ùå 1         | `0, 0.1, 0.5, 0.9999` (**not 1**)   |
| `(0, 1]` | After 0 **up to and including** 1 | ‚ùå 0, ‚úÖ 1         | `0.1, 0.5, 0.9999, 1` (**not 0**)   |
| `(0, 1)` | Between 0 and 1 (exclusive)       | ‚ùå 0 & 1          | `0.1, 0.5, 0.9999` (**not 0 or 1**) |

---

### üîé How to Read:

* **\[ or ]** = "included"
* **( or )** = "excluded"
* So:

  * `[0, 1]` ‚Üí includes both ends
  * `(0, 1)` ‚Üí excludes both ends
